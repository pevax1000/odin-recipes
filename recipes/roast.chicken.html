This is a deliious roast chicken recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 cup of olive oil
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 1 cup of pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Rub chicken with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Place chicken in oven for 1 hour
  4. Remove chicken from oven and let sit for 10 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy!
